About Us

Medical Supply Corporation is landmark in the list of Medical Equipment suppliers in the region. It has started its journey in 2016 as a supplier at a medium scale, but in a short span MSC has proved its magnificent and unique service providing capabilities. Today MSC is successfully giving its services in approximately 20 hospitals / institutes in Karachi, Hyderabad, Shaheed Benazirabad and Larkana . Further, MSC is looking forward to cover other provinces too.

All the policies followed by MSC are stern and impassable with trust in sales and quality of services offered. Medical Supply Corporation aims to be one of the unique and leading firms in list of Medical Sales,Services and Solution provider in the country.

With the strong belief and determination of founder all the employees of MSC are working with all their heart and enthusiasm to make company grow and reach apex. MSC gives prior value to the trust and expectations of our customer and promotes mutual benefit between the vendor and customer.
